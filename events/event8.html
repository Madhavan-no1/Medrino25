Rules and Regulations

    Team Composition

    • Each team must consist of **4 members.**

    Event Flow

    • Round 1: Duration - **45 minutes**
    • A problem statement will be given along with a chart and sketch materials.
    • Teams must present their solution within **3 minutes**.
    • **5-8 teams** will be shortlisted for the final round.
    • Round 2: Duration - **30 minutes**
    • A new problem statement will be given with a chart and sketch.
    • Teams will again have **3 minutes** to present their final solution.
