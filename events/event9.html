Rules and Regulations

  • Participants must enter the allocated venue on time.
  • The event consists of **three rounds**.

    • Round 1: Fandom Quiz

    • Participants will answer **fandom-related questions** from various popular universes.
    • Participants will be selected for the next round based on scores.

    Round 2: Image-Based Guessing

    • Selected participants will have to guess the correct word based on an image or picture.
    • Based on scores, participants will be grouped into teams.

    Round 3: Find the Impostor

    • Teams must identify the **impostor** among them based on a vague description given by each player.
    • The impostor must be found **within one minute**.
    • The **winner will be selected based on scores.**
