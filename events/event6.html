Rules and Regulations

    General Rules

    • Each team should not contain more than 3 members (minimum of 2 members).
    • One member cannot be in more than one team.
    • The paper submitted should exactly be in IEEE format.
    • Kindly follow the font size, style, and spacing according to the format.

    Event Flow

    • The competition will be conducted in 2 rounds.
    • The first round requires participants to submit their abstract on or before 26 FEB.
    • Abstract should not exceed 250 words (refer to IEEE abstract format).
    • The competition will be conducted in the allotted venue.
    • Each participant will be given 7 minutes for presentation and 2 minutes for Q&A.
    • Selected abstracts will qualify for the final round on the symposium day.
    • Finalists must submit their full paper on 27 FEB for plagiarism evaluation.
    • Teams must bring both soft copies (USB drive) and hard copies (stick file).
    • Plagiarism limit: 10%.
    • The jury's decision is final.
