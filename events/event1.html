Rules and Regulations

  • Timings are 10-10 minutes with a 2-minute break.
  • ID cards are a must for verification.
  • Full stockings, shin pads, goalkeeper gloves, and football shoes are required.
  • No trainer shoes are allowed.
  • A knockout system will be followed.
  • Referees' decisions are final.
  • Any kind of intentional physical contact or use of foul language will lead to disqualification of the team.
  • Accessories like chains, rings, and bracelets are not allowed.
