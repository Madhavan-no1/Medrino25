Rules and Regulations

    Game Overview:

    • This event is about two people fighting for the last life jacket on a sinking ship.
    • Each participant must prove their worthiness through argument to secure the life jacket.
    • The winner, deemed the most worthy, will get the life jacket and "survive."
    • Participants will be assigned famous personalities and must argue in character.
