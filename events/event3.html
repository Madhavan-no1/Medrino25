Open Mic

Rules and Regulations

  • Participants must register in advance or on the spot to secure their slot.
  • Each performer gets a fixed time (e.g., 3-5 minutes) to perform.
  • Any form of performance is allowed (e.g., poetry, storytelling, stand-up comedy, music, etc.), unless explicitly restricted.
  • Content should be respectful and suitable for a general audience. Hate speech, offensive language, or discriminatory remarks are strictly prohibited.

Performance Guidelines

  • Participants must use the provided mic and stage setup. Any additional equipment must be pre-approved.
  • Participants must be punctual and ready when called on stage.
  • Participants should not engage in activities that may disrupt the event, such as excessive shouting or physical stunts that could cause harm.
  • Audience interaction should be positive and engaging.

Judging & Disqualification

  • Performances may be evaluated based on originality, engagement, and overall impact.
  • Any participant violating the rules may be disqualified without prior notice.
