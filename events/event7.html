Rules and Regulations

    Team Composition

    • The event is open to all departments.
    • Each team must consist of two participants.
    • Teams can be pre-formed or assigned on the spot.

    Rounds and Elimination

    • The event consists of two consecutive rounds.
    • Teams will be filtered out based on performance in each round.

    Round Details

    • Round 1: UNO Quiz Challenge
    • 36 teams will be divided across 6 tables.
    • Each table will have 6 teams competing against each other.
    • Teams will play UNO while answering 30 quiz questions of varying difficulty (Easy, Intermediate, Hard).
    • The team earning the highest points at each table will qualify.
    • 6 teams will move to the next round.
    • Round 2: Surprise Event
    • The 6 winning teams from Round 1 will compete in a surprise challenge.
    • The details of this round will be revealed on the spot.
    • The final winning team will be determined based on performance in this challenge.
