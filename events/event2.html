Rules and Regulations

  • Team Composition: The event is open to all departments, and participants must form teams of two.
  • Rounds and Elimination: The event consists of three consecutive rounds, with teams being filtered out after each round.
  • Round Details:
    • Round 1: Crack the Code - Teams identify hidden words from clues. 8 teams qualify for the next round.
    • Round 2: Arrange and Conquer - Teams solve numerical clues and arrange them. 4 teams move to the final round.
    • Round 3: Quote URSelf - Teams deduce questions from images and bid on the number of answers they can provide.
